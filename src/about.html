Liftosaur

The best weightlifting routines from r/fitness and r/weightroom, packed into a featureful and easy to use web app.

Check it out

Features

  • Built-in workout routines

    Choose from multiple precreated workouts, that helped thousands of Reddit lifters become stronger.

  • Workout history

    All your workouts will be recorded, and you'll be able to edit any recorded workout.

  • Tracking workout progress

    Log all the sets you accomplished. Also, there's rest timer and plates calculator for your convenience.

    Plates Calculator
    Rest Timer

  • Custom workouts

    You can customize any workout in any way you want, and also create yours.

  • Add any logic to your workouts

    Using built-in script language, you can customize progressions and deloads in any way you want.

  • Cloud Storage

    If you log in using your Google account, your history will be stored in the cloud.

    Log in here

  • Graphs

    You can visually check your progress on main lifts.

Check it out

It's not a native mobile app, it's a web app. So, you visit and work with it just like with any site, open it e.g. in mobile Safari or Chrome, but you can add it to your phone home screen, and then it will behave like a regular app.
